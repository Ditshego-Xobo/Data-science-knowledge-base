\# Common Mistakes in Data Science



Beginners in Data Science often make mistakes that can affect the quality of their analysis.



\## Common Mistakes



\- Not cleaning data properly  

\- Using incorrect data  

\- Ignoring missing values  

\- Overcomplicating analysis  

\- Not understanding the data  



\## Why These Mistakes Matter



These mistakes can lead to wrong conclusions and poor decision-making.



\## How to Avoid Them



\- Always clean your data  

\- Double-check your work  

\- Keep your analysis simple  

\- Understand your dataset before analysing  



\## Conclusion



Avoiding common mistakes helps improve the accuracy and reliability of your results.
