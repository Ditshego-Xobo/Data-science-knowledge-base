\# Why Data Science Matters



Data Science plays an important role in today’s world because organisations rely on data to make decisions.



\## Better Decision Making

Companies use data science to make informed decisions instead of guessing.



\## Real-World Applications

\- Businesses analyse customer data to improve services  

\- Hospitals use data to improve patient care  

\- Banks detect fraud using data analysis  



\## Efficiency and Productivity

Data science helps automate processes, saving time and reducing errors.



\## Conclusion

Data Science is important because it helps organisations solve problems and improve performance using data.
